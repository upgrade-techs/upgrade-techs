Mansfield Gardens Creative Brief

Creative Brief

Project Background

  • Client: Mansfield Gardens
  • Product/Service: Gardening Products and Services
  • SWOT: SWOT Analysis
  • Existing Research: The client is looking for a website redesign and so we have consulted with them and provided this information here. We strive to make customers happy and according to our research, the client also enjoys its customers and would like to offer promotions which we are currently working towards implementing.
  • Date: March 6, 2022

Overview

  • Project type: To redesign the existing website for Mansfield Gardens.
  • Reason for project: We are designing a website so customers that are interested in Mansfield Gardens can find information on the buisness.
  • Opportunities: We would like to capitolize on the opportunity of those interested in gardening and looking implement their own home garden.

Drivers

  • Goals: Our goal is to create an easy to understand website for interested users, they can have a look at the various types of flowers we have, get a quote as well as place orders online, from anywhere at anytime!
  • Top three objectives: We have three main objectives: Educating users on the business; we want our customers to be satisfied with their purchases from Mansdield Gardens. Our website will show descriptions of the flowers which will help users decide which flower they are interested in. Secondly, we want to redesign the website in order for it to be visually appealing. The more appealing the website is, the more users will come. Thirdly, with all the updates on our website, we want to increase the foot traffic on our website. Our main goal is to increase sales, which the website will do using our promotions.
  • Project length: Three months.
  • Length of time until goal is achieved: The client has stated they would like the website up before Summer as they offer seasonal products.

Audience

  • Target Audience:
    • Age: 26-35 years old
    • Sex: Both male and female
    • Education: Minimum High school Diploma
    • Technology competency: Mobile first
    • Race: All races welcome
    • Income level: $26,000-$30,000 minimum
    • Other: To reach International cultures
  • What do they think of us? We sell home grown flowers for indoor, outdoor and offices. We are steroid free, pest free.
  • Why should they care about our project and products? We put time and effort into bringing in new products. We are successful at customer satisfaction. Customers are interested in caring about our products when they are satisfied.

Competitors

  • Direct competitors:
    • Calloways
    • Home Depot
    • Lowes
    • Ricos Plant Farm
  • Indirect competitors:
    • Smaller plant nurseries
    • Facebook Marketplace
  • What efforts could we duplicate from our competitors?
    • We provide education services to our clients.
    • We are pest free.
    • Our flowers are grown naturally, without any use of any steroids.
  • What makes us unique from the competition? We are a family owned business that welcomes newcomers. We also use Non-GMO and organic products as well as heriloom open-pollinated plant.

Tone

  • What tone should we use to communicate to our target audience? Why? The group that is increasing are millennials and are currently out of touch with them. The tone should be friendly and not old fashioned.
  • What adjectives describe the feeling we are trying to project or our approach? To approach millennials, we need to be loyal, easy-going, and enthusiastic.

Message

  • What message are we trying to convey with this site? Would like customers to feel inviting when visiting the store or the website. If customers come in looking to buy one plant, we would like to let them leave double the amount and with more knowledge
  • Is copyright provided by the client? No copyright, free to reuse and redesign.

Visuals

  • Are we developing new images or picking up existing ones? Yes we are developing new images. A logo and favicon, as well as adding a variety of appealing visuals and graphics to the website.
  • If we are creating them, who, what, where are we photographing or illustrating? And why? We are creating a new logo and favicon. The client wants to upgrade their logo message and feels its outdated and wants to attract more customers.

Details

  • What information on the site must be included?
    • The Mission statement
    • A family owned operation.
    • The signup subscription.
    • Contact information.
    • Seasonal offerings.
  • List of deliverables? Preconceived ideas? Format parameters? Limitations and restrictions? Timeline, schedule, budget?
    • List of Deliverables: The creative brief, swot analysis, and sitemap are the deliverables at this point in time.
    • Preconceived ideas: There are no preconceived ideas as the client wants to stay in touch with their audience and attract new gardening customers.
    • Format parameters: The parameters are that we redesign a mobile first technology website.
    • Limitations and restrictions: The client does not have any limitations or restrictions. We are approved to develop and complete redesign for the website.
    • Timeline, schedule, budget: The timeline and schedule is by summer since seasonal offerings are important. The budget at this point is still on the table.

People

  • Reporting to: Upgrade Techs | Project Manager
  • Approving work: Upgrade Techs | Project Manager
  • Who needs to be informed of our progress: Project Manager | Client
